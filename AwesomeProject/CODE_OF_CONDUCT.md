# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a respectful and welcoming experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Accept feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users.
- Avoid harassment, insults, and discriminatory language.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that do not align with this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban from participation.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior privately to the maintainers. Include relevant links, screenshots, and context where possible.
